FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any added travel or service fees?

No, the price you see is the price you pay. No surprise fees—ever.

Do you move furniture?

We move small items (couches, loveseats), but not large sectionals or beds. Please move those before we arrive.

How long will the appointment take?

Most cleanings take 1.5 to 2 hours depending on the size and services.

What is the dry time?

1–2 hours for most carpets depending on material and environment.

Can I substitute a rug for a room?

Yes, as long as it’s standard size and not wool or too lightweight.

What payment forms do you accept?

Cash or credit card at time of service. We can also text a secure payment link.

Do I need to be home?

No, but we need access. Many clients provide garage codes or let us in via neighbors.

What if I have pet stains?

Choose the Pet Pro or Ultimate package—both include enzymes and deodorizing.

Do you clean Base Housing?

No, we currently do not service on-base military housing.
